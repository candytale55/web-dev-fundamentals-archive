</li>
<li>

<li>
<div><em>Example:</em> ""He was convicted of murder for planning and carrying out the attack.""</div>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<div><strong>Manslaughter</strong> – The unlawful killing of a person <strong>without premeditation</strong> or malicious intent. It can be:</div>
<ul>
<li>
<div><strong>Voluntary manslaughter</strong> – A killing committed in the heat of the moment (e.g., after provocation).</div>

<li>

</li>
<li>

</li>
</ul>
</li>

<li>

</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
