Vampattack!

Vampattack! is a pulse-pounding action-romance set in a gritty, steampunk world overrun by bloodthirsty vampires. As the undead swarm from the shadows, a fearless couple of rogue vampire hunters stand their ground—armed with gears, grit, and a forbidden love. With danger closing in and sparks flying, Vampattack! delivers stylish combat, smoldering chemistry, and gothic thrills in every frame.
